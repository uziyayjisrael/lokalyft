Privacy Policy

Lokalyft Passenger App

Effective Date: February 10, 2026

Last Updated: February 10, 2026

Lokalyft ("we", "us", or "our") operates the Lokalyft Passenger mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Data

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Phone number, name, email address, and profile photo when you create an account.
  • Google Account Data: If you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, and profile picture from your Google account.
  • Payment Information: Payment method details processed through our third-party payment provider (Paystack). We do not store your full card details.
  • Communication Data: Any messages or feedback you send to us through the App.
Automatic Data

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Location Data: Real-time GPS location to connect you with nearby vehicles and provide ride services. Location is collected only while you are actively using the App.
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers, and app version.
  • Usage Data: App interaction data, ride history, booking patterns, and feature usage analytics.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Ride Services: To facilitate ride bookings, connect you with vehicle owners, and process payments.
  • Account Management: To create, maintain, and secure your account.
  • Communication: To send ride confirmations, updates, and important service notifications.
  • Safety & Security: To verify identities, prevent fraud, and ensure the safety of passengers and drivers.
  • Improvement: To analyze usage patterns and improve our services, features, and user experience.
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws, regulations, and legal processes.

3. Information Sharing

We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • Vehicle Owners/Drivers: Your name and pickup/drop-off location are shared with the vehicle owner when you book a ride.
  • Payment Processors: Payment data is shared with Paystack to process transactions securely.
  • Service Providers: We use Firebase (Google) for authentication, data storage, and analytics.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or governmental authority.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

4. Data Storage & Security

  • Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase infrastructure with encryption at rest and in transit.
  • We implement industry-standard security measures including secure authentication, access controls, and regular security reviews.
  • While we strive to protect your data, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. Ride history and payment records are retained for a period necessary for legal and accounting purposes. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data and account.
  • Portability: Request your data in a portable format.
  • Withdrawal of Consent: Withdraw consent for data processing at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email below.

7. Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for use by children under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that we have collected data from a child, we will take steps to delete it promptly.

8. Third-Party Services

Our App integrates with the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Google Maps: For location and mapping services.
  • Google Sign-In: For authentication.
  • Firebase: For data storage, authentication, and analytics.
  • Paystack: For payment processing.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new policy within the App and updating the "Last Updated" date. Your continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
